Bio

Poison oak is a beautiful plant that grows throughout the Pacific northwest. It has big scalloped green oak leaves that look very similar to most of the native plants that grow throughout Oregon. Unfortunately for humans if you come in to contact with it you will develop a rash within 8 hours to 2 days. This guide was made to help indentify it in it's nautural habitat.
