Poison Oak Field Guide

Bio

Poison oak is a beautiful plant that grows throughout the Pacific northwest. It has scalloped leaves that grows in sets of three. The leaves look very similar to oak leaves. If you come in to contact with posion oak, there is a 75% chance that you will develop a rash. The rash will usually develop within 8 hours to 5 days. This guide was made to help indentify poison oak in it's nautural habitat.
